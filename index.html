About

Hey there, my name is Taylor! I come from a background in the TV & Film industry with 6 years working as a Grip on various TV shows and movies based in PDX and the surrounding areas. I’ve always had an affinity for computer technologies and design whether that’s in the form of graphic design, coding, or a combination of those, like video games. Since 2022 I have taken a step back from working in the Film industry to pursue my passion and teach myself the fundamentals of coding.

I started with JavaScript but quickly realized that I enjoyed being able to work on the visual aspects of projects and started learning HTML and CSS in order to follow the path of becoming a front-end developer. I’ve taught myself everything I know through courses on LinkedIn Learning, freeCodeCamp, and various tutorials on YouTube.

I’m always seeking to improve my skills as a developer and a graphic designer and look forward to documenting my achievements on this site!
